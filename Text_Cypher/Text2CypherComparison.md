## 3 different types of Text 2 Cypher
   A. Dynamic Cypher Implementation
   B. Text2Cypher 
   C. Cypher Templates

## Dynamic Cypher Implementation
   

## Cypher Templates: 
   like having different model or toys needed to assemble
   a solution for the problem. In case of not having enough
   models or templates, you cannot solve the problem.
    

## Text2Cypher: 
    An AI Model like an LLM such as LLama-3 when sufficiently
    finetuned performs the translation of text to cypher 

    Adv: No hard templates required
    Disav: If not well finetuned, it can fail miserably

   This is the most flexible version of the 3.
